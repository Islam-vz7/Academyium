Our Mission

To advance the practice of emergency and critical care medicine by providing high-quality, accessible, and hands-on procedural training to medical professionals from around the world.

Who We Are

AEMIS was founded by a group of passionate emergency physicians, anaesthetists, and trauma surgeons who recognized a need for more realistic and intensive procedural training. We believe that confidence and competence are built through deliberate practice. Our courses are designed to bridge the gap between theory and real-world clinical practice, creating a safe yet challenging environment for learners to hone their skills.

We are committed to excellence, innovation, and fostering a collaborative learning community.
