Course Programme

Our intensive two-day programme is structured to maximize hands-on learning, covering a wide array of critical emergency procedures. Each day combines short, focused lectures with extensive practical skill stations.

Day 1: Airway, Thoracic & Vascular Access

08:00 - 08:30 | Registration & Welcome Coffee

Meet the faculty and fellow participants.

08:30 - 09:00 | Course Introduction & Aims

Setting the stage for an intensive learning experience.

09:00 - 12:00 | Skill Station 1: Advanced Airway Management

Video laryngoscopy, surgical cricothyrotomy, fiberoptic intubation.

12:00 - 13:00 | Lunch

13:00 - 16:00 | Skill Station 2: Thoracic Procedures

Chest drain insertion (Seldinger & open), thoracentesis, emergency thoracotomy.

16:00 - 17:00 | Skill Station 3: Vascular Access

Ultrasound-guided central line, intraosseous access.

17:00 - 17:30 | Q&A and Day 1 Wrap-up

Day 2: Trauma, Ultrasound & Advanced Skills

08:30 - 09:00 | Morning Briefing

Review of Day 1 and overview of Day 2.

09:00 - 12:00 | Skill Station 4: POCUS in Trauma

eFAST scan, basic cardiac echo, vascular assessment.

12:00 - 13:00 | Lunch

13:00 - 16:00 | Skill Station 5: Advanced Surgical Skills

Resuscitative Hysterotomy, Escharotomy, Lateral Canthotomy.

16:00 - 17:00 | Integrated Scenarios

Applying learned skills in high-fidelity simulated trauma scenarios.

17:00 - 17:30 | Course Debrief & Certificates
